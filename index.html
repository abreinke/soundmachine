Storm Symphony

Directions for Generating the Thunderstorm

To start a downpour, press the "r" key.
To create a thunder crack, press the "t" key.
To create hail sounds, press the "h" key.
To create wind, press the "w" key.
To increase the sound volume, press the "a" key.
To decrease the sound volume, press the "d" key.
To stop the storm, press the "p" key.
